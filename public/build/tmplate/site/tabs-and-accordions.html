  • How do you calculate your rates?
  • What are the usual methods of freight payment?
  • Where are you located?
  • What are your areas of operation?
  • What foreign languages does your staff speak?
  • What are your hours of operation?
Our rates are time based. We charge by the hour with a 2 hour minimum. Time is calculated garage to garage in 15 minute increments. The price is not affected by the number of passengers.
Most freight payments are done with a Company check. However you can also pay with wire transfer or credit card (subject to administrative fee). Payment is sent right around the time the freight is due to arrive, clear customs and be released.
Transitec's offices are located in Flushing, Queens, in New York City. Our bases of operations for logistics services are in Queens and the Bronx.
We are available for trips throughout the NY Metro area and out of town trips up to a 250 mile radius.
We speak Spanish, Chinese, Russian, and Korean.
Our office is open 8am to 7pm Mon. thru Fri. and 8am to 3pm on Sat. & Sun. Our main phone 718-507-0500 is answered 24 hours. With sufficient notice our vehicles are available 7 days a week 24 hours a day, with the exception of special holidays.
